While Murder Rates have exceeded records in the US, The Deadliest Region for Trans Murder is Improving

By Sandra Lopez

December 15, 2021

Between October 1, 2020 through September 30, 2021, 375 trans and gender-diverse individuals were murdered worldwide. According to a report released by Transrespect Versis Transphobia worldwide, the region of Latin America accounts for 70% of these deaths.
